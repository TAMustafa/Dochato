How Dochato Works

  1. Emma from Venezuella want to raise money to buy medecine and creates a fund raising campaign on dochato.com.
  2. Emma writes a brief describtion and desired funding goal on dochato.com for donators to react.
  3. Kirk and Justin like to help Emma and donate STEEM / SBD to her. As a reward dochato.com will send them DCT Token to their STEEM powered multi wallet.
  4. Emma received 100% of the donations without any fees thanks to the STEEM blockchain. Kirk and Justin feel good about their donation and in addtion received DCT Token which likely increase in value overtime.
